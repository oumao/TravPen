Tours and Travels


MALDIVES

View details

The Maldives is a real paradise for tourists with some of the most luxurious hotels in the world. It is known for the underwater world, sun-kissed beaches, palm-fringed islands, crystalline lagoons and clean waters. Maldivian reef is a spot known for hammerhead sharks going for breakfast and you can also swim with whale sharks. The Maldives is a Muslim country and has embraced its culture linking Maldivian mosques to one of the most popular sights on the islands.


COSTA RICA

View details

Costa Rica has a large number of natural attractions with almost a quarter of the country covered by national reserves. Despite the small size of Costa Rica, it has various landscapes from humid rainforests to dry landscapes, from high mountains to swampy lowlands. The country also has active volcanos that are not dangerous to visit and is known for a variety of wildlife, distinct culture and a surplus of adventure activities.Â 


UGANDA

View details

Uganda does not have too much tourism activity going on hence can easily be visited on a low budget. Some of the tourist sites in Uganda are Murchison Falls National Park, Kidepo Valley National Park, Mgahinga Gorilla National Park and Ajai Game Reserve.

FIJI

View details

Fiji has interesting hiking activities in the national parks and surfing with big ocean waves. This country is known for its volcanic landscape and attractive beaches that give a heavenly atmosphere. Fiji is made up of 332 islands, located in the Pacific Ocean with only a part of it inhabited.


MYANMAR

View details

The time to visit this country is during the dry season. Myanmar country has Inle Lake where local fishing goes on and can be interesting to watch. Myanmar has perfectly preserved its local traditions and wild nature that you can see.
